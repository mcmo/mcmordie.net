I am teaching myself to code by making one website per weekday!

Recently I have been trying to learn how to program but have had limited success. Most of my training has been reading books, taking online classes, and working through tutorials.

Now I am taking a different approach. I read about Jennifer Dewalt, who created 180 websites in 180 days. The idea is that you learn and retain information better by actually building something yourself. So I will use her daily website ideas as a guide and see what I can do!

My plan is to build a website each day. I want to focus on Javascript and eventually learn related technologies such as Node.JS, Meteor, MongoDB, etc...
